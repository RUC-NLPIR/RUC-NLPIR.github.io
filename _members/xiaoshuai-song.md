Song Xiaoshuai is currently a PhD student at the Gaoling School of Artificial Intelligence, Renmin University of China, under the supervision of Prof. Zhicheng Dou and Prof. Ji-Rong Wen. He earned his bachelor’s degree (2022) and Master’s degree (2024) in Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications. His research interests include agents and tool learning based on large language models.
